about us

we take care of your healthy life

ProClinic Hospital started in 2018 with 100 beds with a vision of providing quality and affordable healthcare to citizens of Dombivli, Kalyan and Thane. Over period of 15 years, it has transformed into a centre of excellence and NABH accredited Multi-speciality 250 bedded hospital, commanding the trust of patients and commitment of its team.

Our facilities are strongly backed by the support of State-of-the-art Operation Theatres (OTs) equipped with the latest equipment and laminar airflow systems, to maintain internationally accepted sterility norms that minimize the risk of infection, 128 slice CT scan, high-end 1.5 Tesla MRI, Dual Energy Linear Accelerator (Radiation Unit), State-of-the-art Cath lab which will provide Diagnostic & Therapeutic procedures covering Angioplasty, Stenting, Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump Insertion and Pacemaker Insertions, high-end Gastro-endoscopy Unit and other latest modalities.
